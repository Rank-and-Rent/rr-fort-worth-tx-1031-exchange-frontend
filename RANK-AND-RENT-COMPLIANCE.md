# Rank and Rent Compliance Guidelines

## Core Principles

This site routes inquiries to our chosen fulfillment partner for 1031 exchange advisory and property identification support. All content must comply with rank and rent best practices.

## Content Rules

### Never Claim Past Projects or Specific Clients
- Do not reference specific past projects
- Do not name specific clients
- Do not use client testimonials or reviews

### Frame Scenarios as Examples
- All scenarios are examples of the type of engagement we can handle
- Use language like "Example of the type of engagement we can handle"
- Invite contact to share real references upon request

### Do Not Fabricate Information
- Do not make up timelines or outcomes
- Do not create false testimonials
- Do not use review stars or badges without sources
- Do not claim specific results or guarantees

### Tax and Legal Disclaimers
- 1031 defers income tax on qualifying real property
- 1031 does not remove transfer or documentary taxes
- All content is educational only
- Not tax, legal, or investment advice

### Securities Disclaimers
- If DST or TIC is mentioned, include a securities disclaimer
- We do not sell securities
- We provide introductions to licensed providers only

## Footer Disclosure

All pages must include:

```
Disclosure: This site routes inquiries to our chosen fulfillment partner for 1031 exchange advisory and property identification support.
```

## Verification Requirements

- DO NOT MAKE UP INFORMATION WITHOUT VERIFYING IT TO BE TRUE, ESPECIALLY FOR LOCATIONS
- Verify all location data including population, employers, and business districts
- Use current data from 2024 or 2025 sources
- Confirm map locations and radius information

## Language Guidelines

- Use plain English
- No contractions
- No em dashes
- Short sentences
- Technical accuracy
- Rank and rent compliant language only
